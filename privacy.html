Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

All app data, including your progress and preferences, is stored locally on your device. This data is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed.

Our apps do not integrate with third-party services that collect user data. There are no ads, no tracking SDKs, and no external analytics tools.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Our apps are safe for users of all ages.
